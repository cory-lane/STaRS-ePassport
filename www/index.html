About ePassport

Created by: Cory Johnson && Ryan Muhlbauer

STaRS is an annual event to showcase your science, mathematics and technology activities and research. Students and faculty enjoy science demos, games and poster presentations. Graduate and professional schools also attend STaRS to discuss academic programs and admission requirements. Also, SST clubs host booths and welcome student registrations. Thanks for coming and enjoy participating in STaRS!
